About The Coffee House

The Coffee House started its first outlet in the year 2016 and year after receiving a tremendous response from the customers the Founders has decided to start a chain in Boston city. Bieng, the first of its own kind of Cafe, The Coffee house provides its customer a soothing ambience. we provide free wifi to our customers so that their work continous with their coffee. we also provide self-service to the customers do that they can place their orders when they are ready. We do have snacks like burger, pizza, sandwiches but coffee is our speciality.
Apart from this, we also deliver iced coffee to your door steps. It is really easy to place your order online using our site. Once you registered to our website, you will receive a special discount offers in weekly newsletter. We are giving new experience to our customers by providing happy hour services, where they get a one plus one offer on any beverages of their choice. To cheer up our customer we have a special event every evening 7pm to 9m.
