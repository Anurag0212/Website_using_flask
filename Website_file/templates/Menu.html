Specialty Drinks

Hot Drinks

  • Homemade Chai – We make it in house with black tea, spices, and honey so its never too sweet and is just spicy enough.
  • Hot Cider – Each fall and winter, we source locally grown and pressed apple cider.
  • Spiced Americano – A bag of our Cardamon Cinnamon tea added to a traditional americano.
  • London Fog – A rich combination of steamed milk, earl grey tea, and vanilla.
  • Matcha – Japanese matcha powder, blended with milk. Available hot or cold.
  • Homemade Hot Chocolate – Blended in house with Dutch-process cocoa.
  • Mexican Hot Chocolate – Our house-made cocoa spiced with chipotle and cinnamon.
  • Almond Joy – Our house made hot chocolate with almond and coconut. Better than the candy!
  • Vietnamese Coffee – Available hot and iced, it’s a rich combination of dark roast and sweetened condensed milk.

Iced Drinks

  • Malted Cold Brew – Our concoction of concentrated cold brew, milk, cream, malt and a touch of sugar.
  • Lemonade – Always squeezed fresh to order.
  • Cranberry Cooler – A refreshing blend of 7-herb hibiscus tea and cranberry juice.
  • Iced Tea – In the summer time we offer up to six varieties of freshly brewed, unsweetened iced tea.
  • Italian Soda – Sweet syrups mixed with crisp sparkling water. Built to order. Make your own flavor!
